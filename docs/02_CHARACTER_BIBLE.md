# 02 Character Bible — 《海洋戰紀》

## Hero faction

### 雙帶武士
Role: Northern border samurai hero.

Visual identity:

- Yellow clownfish-like body
- Black-gold samurai armor
- Large gold kabuto helmet
- Serious expression
- Uses katana / sword techniques

Story status:

- Initially guards the northern border.
- In 異變追跡, clears a battlefield of garbage anemones.
- Faces the fused giant garbage anemone.
- After victory, heads toward 食人鯊戰神石像 seeking martial breakthrough.

Game role:

- First playable character.
- Balanced melee fighter.
- Strong slash, dodge, and charged special attack.

Suggested abilities:

- Basic Slash
- Dash Cut
- Guard Step
- Golden Arc Slash
- Core Breaker special

### 黑豹忍者
Role: Stealth hero in the dark-current line.

Visual identity:

- Black ninja outfit
- Masked face
- White markings
- Agile, compact body
- Twin swords or stealth blades

Story status:

- Initially training in the dark-current area with 透紅浪人.
- Discovers mutation during training.
- After clearing anomalies, enters the temple to seek revelation / origin clues.

Game role:

- Future playable stealth/speed character.
- High evasion, rapid strikes, trap disarm.

### 透紅浪人
Role: Red ronin hero.

Visual identity:

- Red clownfish-like body
- Worn red ronin outfit
- Serious and silent expression
- Sword user

Story status:

- Training with 黑豹忍者.
- Discovers dark-current mutation.
- Enters the temple to find the origin of the abnormal water current.

Game role:

- Future playable precision melee character.
- Strong counterattacks and single-hit damage.

### 雪印法師
Role: Ice mage hero.

Visual identity:

- Orange-white clownfish with ice armor / ice crown
- Holds ice staff or crystal orb
- Calm, mystical, analytical

Story status:

- Has already blocked 獅子魚 with a large ice-sealing spell.
- Decides with 銀背突擊兵 to go to the ice field / ice temple to seek whale-shark revelation.
- 神諭 has not fully appeared yet.

Game role:

- Future playable support/mage.
- Ice shield, freeze field, slow enemies, revelation scan.

### 銀背突擊兵
Role: Armored assault soldier and escort.

Visual identity:

- Orange clownfish base
- Transparent / silvery combat armor
- Futuristic helmet with goggles
- Rifle / blaster
- Tactical, serious, protective

Important correction:

- The correct design is not a generic soldier. It must have orange fish body, transparent silver armor, front goggles, and rifle.

Story status:

- Supports 雪印法師.
- Helps protect the journey toward the ice region.

Game role:

- Future ranged character.
- Rifle shots, suppression fire, shield deploy, scanner.

### 公子王子
Role: Prince line, travels from the palace toward the tribe.

Visual identity:

- Orange-white prince clownfish
- White/gold royal clothing
- Gentle but brave

Story status:

- After 王女封印, travels to the tribe to seek support.
- Later gets surrounded by enemies.
- Rescued by 鸚哥勇士.

Game role:

- Story-driven support character.
- May unlock tribe alliance.

### 粉紅舞孃
Role: Messenger / dancer line, travels to the coral old street.

Visual identity:

- Pink dancer-like fish
- Elegant, soft, cheerful but brave

Story status:

- Leaves palace for coral old street.
- Arrives and finds the old street already affected by anemone invasion.

Game role:

- Future support / charm / buff character.
- Could provide morale, healing, or crowd-control rhythm mechanics.

### 紅藝伎
Role: Coral old street guardian / performer.

Visual identity:

- Red geisha fish
- Kimono, fan, ornate hairstyle
- Elegant and alert

Story status:

- Present in coral old street.
- Becomes involved when garbage anemones invade.

Game role:

- Future support / ranged fan technique / illusion skill.

### 熊貓相撲
Role: Coral old street defender.

Visual identity:

- Black-white fish pattern
- Sumo styling
- Sturdy, stern, physical defender

Story status:

- Present in old street daily life.
- Defends when garbage anemones appear.

Game role:

- Future tank character.
- Push, slam, guard, body-block.

### 鸚哥勇士
Role: Tribe warrior.

Visual identity:

- Bright red/orange parrotfish warrior
- Spear
- Tribal armor

Story status:

- Rescues 公子王子 when surrounded.
- Opens the tribe line.

Game role:

- Future playable spear character.
- Fast rescue / charge / spear sweep.

### 蘇眉勇士
Role: Tribe warrior and archer.

Visual identity:

- Green-blue wrasse-like warrior
- Bow and tribal gear

Story status:

- Located in the tribe line with 鸚哥勇士.

Game role:

- Future ranged archer.

## Villains and corruption forces

### 鯙鰻軍師
Role: Abyssal strategist / black-tide manipulator.

Visual identity:

- Spotted eel / moray-like body
- Long coiling body
- Dark ornate robe
- Gold chains and luxury accessories
- Sinister intelligent expression

Story status:

- Uses black-tide power.
- Can compress or transform black tide into weapon seeds.
- Works with 六齒魨工程師 to industrialize deep-sea weapons.

Game role:

- Villain commander / stage narrator / later boss.

### 六齒魨工程師
Role: Villain engineer / production-line architect.

Visual identity:

- Yellow-black pufferfish
- Engineer helmet, tools, industrial gear
- Excited / slightly mad expression
- Can enter spike / overload engineering modes

Story status:

- Builds black-tide converter.
- Turns garbage anemones and virus purple urchins into mass-produced abyssal weapons.

Game role:

- Future gadget boss / factory-stage controller.

### 獅子魚武士
Role: Vengeful warrior enemy.

Visual identity:

- Lionfish-inspired warrior
- Red-white spikes/fins
- Samurai-style weaponry
- Fierce and prideful

Story status:

- Previously defeated before obtaining a powerful blade.
- Blocked by 雪印法師’s ice-sealing spell.
- Goes to find 石鯛鐵匠 to refine / sharpen the blade.

Game role:

- Future rival boss.

### 垃圾海葵群
Role: First-stage visible mutation.

Visual identity:

- Translucent blue/turquoise anemone tissue
- Plastic fragments, nets, bottles, marine debris
- Many glossy eyes / bubble-like eyes
- Creeping, multiplying, invasive

Story status:

- First appears as abnormal growth in daily spaces.
- Invades old street, northern battlefield, dark-current area.
- Can fuse into giant boss form.

Game role:

- Basic enemy / environmental hazard / spawn group.

### 巨大垃圾海葵・紫色觸手模式
Role: First major boss for MVP.

Visual identity:

- Huge trash-anemone boss
- Purple translucent tentacles
- Embedded marine debris
- Multiple glossy eyes
- Pollution core
- Long writhing tentacles

Story status:

- Forms when many garbage anemone fragments fuse.
- Blocks the path in the northern battlefield.
- Defeated by 雙帶武士.

Game role:

- First boss.

### 病毒紫海膽
Role: Advanced bioweapon.

Visual identity:

- Purple sea urchin-like creature
- Biohazard tubes, toxic canisters
- Spikes and armored shell
- Angry eyes

Story status:

- Initially should not appear as a fully mature weapon too early.
- In later chapters, created / industrialized by black-tide manipulation and puffer engineering.

Game role:

- Later enemy type / stage hazard.

## Ancient divine traces

### 食人鯊戰神石像
Role: Ancient martial breakthrough line.

Story status:

- The audience may not know the full meaning early.
- After defeating 巨大垃圾海葵, 雙帶武士 goes to the statue to seek martial breakthrough.

Game role:

- Victory unlock story card after Stage 1.

### 鯨鯊輪迴使 / 鯨鯊水晶像
Role: Ice-region revelation line.

Story status:

- 雪印法師 and 銀背突擊兵 decide to seek this神諭, but it has not fully appeared.

### 阿髻鮫陰陽師 / 木雕
Role: Dark-current temple line.

Story status:

- 忍者 and 浪人 enter the temple to seek 起始 / 啟示.
- Full meaning should be revealed later.
