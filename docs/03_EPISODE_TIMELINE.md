## 1. 邪神降臨
Status: existing / foundational.

Purpose:

- Introduce abyssal divine threat.
- Establish deep-sea邪神 atmosphere.
- Suggest black tide and corruption are not ordinary environmental problems.

## 2. 冰晶王女
Status: completed.

Purpose:

- 王女 freezes / seals herself.
- 王城 enters silence.
- 公子小丑 did not strongly intervene in the final version.
- The event becomes the center from which later branches spread.

Key story state after episode:

- 王城 crisis has happened.
- Outside regions mostly do not know.
- The world has not yet fully reacted.

## 3. 遠古傳說
Status: completed.

Purpose:

- Establish ancient divine traces and mythic background.
- Prepare later story lines such as 食人鯊戰神石像, 鯨鯊輪迴使, and dark-current temple revelations.
- Give the world a deeper history beyond the immediate mutation crisis.

## 4. 鸚哥勇士的崛起
Status: completed.

Purpose:

- Establish 鸚哥勇士 and the tribe / rescue line.
- Prepare the later 公子王子 rescue branch.
- Show that non-palace regions have their own heroes and combat traditions.

Note:

- The imported filename uses `堀起`, but canon title should be `崛起`.

## 5. 殘光集結
Status: completed / corrected interpretation.

Purpose:

- Shows daily life / branch positions after王女事件.
- Not all characters know the crisis.
- 王子 and 舞孃 start carrying the message outward.

Important canon correction:

- 北境 characters do not yet reveal full食人鯊石像 meaning.
- 古廟 characters are not yet searching; they may just be practicing.
- 冰原神諭 is not yet revealed.
- 老街 may still appear normal.

Core line:

> 災難已經發生，但世界還不知道。

## 6. 異變蔓延
Status: completed / usable.

Purpose:

- First visible mutation enters daily life.
- Garbage anemones invade or begin invading the coral old street.
- 舞孃 reaches old street and discovers mutation.
- 雪印法師 blocks 獅子魚 with ice.
- 鯙鰻軍師 begins to reveal black-tide manipulation.
- 獅子魚 later seeks 鐵匠 to improve blade.

Core line:

> 王城的消息還沒傳開，異變已先抵達。

## 7. 異變追跡
Status: next target / MVP game title.

Purpose:

- Branches become active.
- Heroes start tracing mutation source and seeking answers.

Planned sequence:

1. 忍者與浪人練功，發現異變。
2. 清除異變後，前往暗流神廟尋找起始 / 啟示。
3. 雪印法師與銀背突擊兵對話，提出前往冰原尋找鯨鯊神諭。
4. 雙帶武士在北境戰場橫掃大量垃圾海葵。
5. 海葵殘骸融合成巨大垃圾海葵。
6. 雙帶武士激戰巨大海葵並斬開核心。
7. 通往食人鯊戰神石像的道路開啟。
8. 片尾接深淵兵器量產開始。
9. 末尾或下段：公子王子被包圍，被鸚哥勇士拯救。

Important title decision:

- Do not call this chapter 神諭與兵器, because神諭 has not yet fully appeared and兵器 production is only at the end.
- Best title: **異變追跡**.

## 8. 深淵兵器
Status: next major villain-side chapter.

Purpose:

- 鯙鰻軍師 uses black tide as controllable force.
- 六齒魨工程師 builds production line.
- Garbage anemones and virus purple urchins become mass-produced weapons.

Potential structure:

1. 黑暗高塔啟動
2. 鯙鰻軍師：黑潮鍛種
3. 六齒魨工程師：深淵量產線
4. 兵器外送 / 釋放
5. 軍師與工程師分工定型

Core line:

> 黑潮，不只是在蔓延。它正在被製造成兵器。

## 9. 赤潮刀鋒 / 鐵匠線
Status: future chapter.

Purpose:

- 獅子魚 seeks the鐵匠 to refine blade.
- The blade’s history and danger become central.
- 鐵匠 faces moral pressure.

Possible titles:

- 赤潮刀鋒
- 鐵匠之罪
- 刀鳴再起

## 10. 各地正式開戰
Status: future expansion.

Potential playable stages:

- Dark-current temple exploration

- Tribe rescue stage
- Abyssal factory infiltration
- Lionfish rival battle
