# 01 Story Bible — 《海洋戰紀》Current Canon

## Series essence
《海洋戰紀》 is a chibi fantasy underwater saga about ocean kingdoms, ancient divine traces, ecological mutation, and political/military conflict under the sea.

The story uses cute Q版 characters, but the dramatic structure is serious:

- 王國危機
- 支線分流
- 黑潮污染
- 古代神像 / 神諭
- 深淵兵器量產
- 英雄突破與反擊

## Current major arcs

### 1. 邪神降臨
Purpose:

- Introduces the deep-sea邪神 force.
- Establishes water-jellyfish deity imagery and the threat of black-tide corruption.
- Sets the atmosphere for a larger abyssal conflict.

Core idea:

> The enemy is not just a monster. It is a system of corruption, belief, and control.

### 2. 冰晶王女
Purpose:

- Central kingdom crisis.
- 王女 uses ice-crystal power and becomes sealed / frozen.
- 王城 enters silence.
- The outside world does not immediately understand the crisis.

Important note:

- 公子王子 did not strongly intervene in the finished version.
- The emotional center is the王女’s self-sealing and the kingdom’s unresolved crisis.

### 3. 殘光集結
Purpose:

- Shows the world after the王女 incident.
- Not all branches know the crisis.
- Most areas are still in daily life or routine duty.
- 王子 and 舞孃 begin carrying the crisis outward.

Correct interpretation:

> 災難已經發生，但世界還不知道。

What the audience should know:

- 王女 has been frozen/sealed.
- 王子 heads toward the tribe.
- 舞孃 heads toward the coral old street.
- Other regions still appear normal.

What should not be revealed too early:

- Full神諭
- Full ancient god explanations
- Complete兵器量產 system
- Mature purple urchin weaponization

### 4. 異變蔓延
Purpose:

- The first visible invasion enters daily spaces.
- Garbage sea anemones begin disrupting the coral old street.
- 舞孃 reaches old street and discovers disaster arrived before the message.
- 鯙鰻軍師 begins to show that black-tide forces may be manipulated.
- 獅子魚 is blocked by雪印法師 and later seeks weapon improvement.

Core phrase:

> 日常被異變悄悄吞入。

### 5. 異變追跡
Purpose:

- Heroes begin tracing the source and looking for answers.
- This is the best current title for the next playable game prototype.

Planned components:

1. 忍者與浪人練功，發現異變。
2. 清除異變後，進入神廟，希望取得啟示。
3. 雪印法師與銀背突擊兵決定前往冰原，尋找鯨鯊神諭。
4. 雙帶武士在戰場橫掃大量垃圾海葵。
5. 海葵融合成巨大垃圾海葵。
6. 雙帶武士激戰並擊敗巨大海葵。
7. 雙帶武士前往食人鯊石像尋求武功突破。
8. 片尾接上深淵兵器量產環節。
9. 末尾可接：公子小丑被敵人包圍，被鸚哥勇士拯救。

Correct title logic:

- 神諭尚未真正展現，只是開始尋找。
- 深淵兵器只是片尾開始量產，還不是本章主內容。
- Therefore this chapter should not be called 神諭與兵器 yet.
- Best title: **異變追跡**.

### 6. 深淵兵器
Purpose:

- Next major villain-side chapter.
- 鯙鰻軍師 uses black-tide power.
- 六齒魨工程師 turns the mutation into an industrial production line.
- 病毒紫海膽 and垃圾海葵 become systematized weapons.

Core phrase:

> 黑潮，不只是在蔓延。它正在被製造成兵器。

## Current narrative rule
The story should progress in stages:

1. Crisis occurs but is not understood.
2. Daily life is disrupted.
3. Heroes trace the source.
4. Ancient traces are sought.
5. Villains industrialize the mutation.
6. Heroes obtain breakthroughs and alliances.
7. War escalates.

Avoid revealing full divine lore or full bioweapon mechanics before the story earns it.
